About us

Rentals in Mysore (Mysuru)

RentOnTrip is a unified rental platform for car rentals, bike rentals, and dress rentals in Mysore. We bring together catalogs from trusted local vendors in one app, so customers can compare options and rent on their terms—by price, model, and whatever else matters to them.

We are based at Rama Iyer Road, Ballal Circle, Mysore, and are an incubated startup at SJCE STEP — focused on building a trusted rental experience for Mysuru and beyond.
