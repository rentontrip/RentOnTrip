Frequently asked questions

Quick answers about RentOnTrip in Mysore

What is RentOnTrip?

RentOnTrip is a unified rental platform for self-drive cars, self-drive bikes, and dress rentals in Mysore (Mysuru). We bring together catalogs from trusted local vendors in one Android app so you can compare options by price, model, and what matters to you.

What can I rent on RentOnTrip?

You can explore self-drive cars, self-drive bikes (two-wheelers), and dresses including traditional and ethnic wear from vendors on the platform. What you see in the app depends on listings from local partners at that time.

How do local vendors work on the platform?

RentOnTrip lists inventory from vendors across Mysore. You browse their offerings in the app, compare choices, and follow the booking steps in the app. Vendors set their own listings; we help you discover and compare them in one place.

How do I download the app?

RentOnTrip is available on Android via the Google Play Store. Tap Download on Play Store in the header of this page or search for RentOnTrip in the Play Store.

How are rental prices decided?

Pricing depends on the vendor, vehicle or outfit, and rental duration (hourly, daily, or occasion-based). Use the app to view current options and compare before you book.

Where is RentOnTrip based?

We are based at Rama Iyer Road, Ballal Circle, Mysore, Karnataka, India. RentOnTrip is an incubated startup at SJCE STEP, Mysore.

Who operates RentOnTrip?

RentOnTrip is operated by Pritha Technologies, focused on building a trusted rental experience for Mysuru and nearby areas.
